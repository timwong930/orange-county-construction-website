# Outreach

## Initial contact
- **Opener:** Hi, I’m Tim — I took a quick look at Orange County Construction and put together a simple idea for how the website could turn more visitors into calls or quote requests.
- **What to mention:**
  - Lead with bigger-project trust: ADUs, remodels, and clear planning/build steps.
  - Mention that the site should make it easy for homeowners to understand what kinds of jobs they take.
- **Ask:** Offer a short screenshot walkthrough of the homepage and contact flow.
- **Tone:** Friendly, local, no-pressure, and short. Keep it practical and specific.
